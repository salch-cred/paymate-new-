# PayMate Brag Plan

## Rubric
1. **Name**: PayMate
2. **One-liner**: A full-stack Web3 invoicing app for independent work — send a payment link, get paid in USDC, and build a portable, on-chain reputation.
3. **Core problem**: Independent contractors doing crypto-paid work today track invoices in DMs and spreadsheets, with no proof of who actually delivers.
4. **Target audience**: Web3 freelancers, contractors, DAOs, and crypto-native teams.
5. **Vibe/Tone**: Polished, professional, and slightly cinematic to emphasize the "winner" status in the GOAT Bootcamp.
6. **Key metric/proof**: Live on GOAT Testnet3, direct non-custodial USDC settlement, zero fake state.
7. **Visual hook**: The dashboard showing "Total Settled" and the green "Paid" status on an invoice.
8. **Call to action**: "Try it now on GOAT Testnet3"
9. **Audio/Music cue**: Fast, upbeat electronic track with heavy drops for highlights.

## Storyboard (15-20s)

**Scene 1: Hook (0s - 3s)**
- *Visual*: Dark background, crisp white typography sliding in.
- *Text*: "Still tracking crypto payments in DMs?"
- *Motion*: Fast zoom out, revealing a messy spreadsheet, which then shatters.

**Scene 2: Reveal (3s - 7s)**
- *Visual*: PayMate Dashboard UI appears cleanly.
- *Text*: "Meet PayMate. Invoicing + On-chain Reputation."
- *Motion*: Pan across the dashboard, highlighting the "Outstanding Balance" and "Settlement Rate".

**Scene 3: Highlight 1 - Settlement (7s - 12s)**
- *Visual*: Split screen showing "Share Link" and "Paid in USDC" popups.
- *Text*: "Get paid instantly. No middlemen. No custody."
- *Motion*: The payment confirmation checkmark scales up with a satisfying bounce.

**Scene 4: Highlight 2 - Reputation (12s - 17s)**
- *Visual*: The ERC-8004 Agent ID badge and trust score animating.
- *Text*: "Every settled job builds your on-chain reputation."
- *Motion*: Score counters rolling up rapidly.

**Scene 5: Outro / CTA (17s - 20s)**
- *Visual*: PayMate logo over GOAT Network badge.
- *Text*: "PayMate. Built for the GOAT Network."
- *Motion*: Clean fade out with URL `paymates.vercel.app`.
